Our Project: FoodFinders

In these unprecedented times, CitiHacks created FoodFinders to help fight hunger and food waste. FoodFinders helps buisnesses parcitipate in that fight by making it to adding their location as a food distribution center. It also helps those in need of finding food by making local food pantries easy to find.
