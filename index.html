Our Project: Food Locator

In these unprecedented times, CitiHacks is creating The Food Finder to help struggling families find local food drives and soup kitchens. The platform could also be expanded to locate other goods such as sanitary products, clothing, and more!

Contact Us

Team CitiHacks welcomes all visitors. We are available to anyone interested in The Food Finder or even joining our team! We are looking for beginners who are interested in learning programming languages such as Java, JavaScript, Python, HTML5, CSS3, and more!
